The Rise of the Agentic Web

The Rise of the Agentic Web: From GenAI Applications to Inter-Agent Protocols The advent of Generative AI (GenAI) marked a significant shift in application development. Initially, we focused on building applications powered by large language models, enabling tasks like content generation, summarization, and code assistance. These applications were primarily monolithic, relying on single models to perform complex tasks. As the field matured, we recognized the limitations of this approach. Complex tasks often require a combination of skills and knowledge domains, which a single model might not efficiently handle.
